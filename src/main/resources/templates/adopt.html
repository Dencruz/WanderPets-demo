They Can't Wait to See You!

Rescued from cruelty and neglect, the dogs are brought in at the Wander Pets shelter for rehabilitation. Upon full recovery, we find them a new home and family to love them furever. #AdoptDontShop

Why Adopt a Rescue Dog?

When you open your doors to welcome a rescue dog as part of your family, you're saving more than just one life. By adopting, you’re helping make space for another animal in need and helping to give them the opportunity to become beloved pets. #AdoptDontShop

How to Adopt from Wander Pets

You’re one step closer toward giving our lovely rescues the life that they deserve. Adopt a rescue now and give them belly rubs everyday!

Application

Accomplish and fill out the Wander Pet Dog Rehoming Application Form to express your desire to adopt a chosen pet.

Evaluation

Upon receipt of the form, we'll schedule an interview to assess if you and your chosen pet are a good match.

Rehoming

Congratulations! You may schedule a pick up of your pet at our shelter or we may deliver it to your address.
